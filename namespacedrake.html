The format is one of:

  • "zero"
  • "cons"
  • "lin(x,...)"
  • "aff(x,...)"
  • "poly(x,...)"
  • "diff(x,...)"
  • "arb(x,...)"
  • "undf(x,...)"

where "x,..." represents a comma-separated list of the variables combined by the form.
